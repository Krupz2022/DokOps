# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

DokOps is an open, welcoming project. Everyone who participates — through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other channel — is expected to treat others with respect and professionalism.

**We expect:**
- Clear, constructive communication
- Patience with contributors of all experience levels
- Feedback directed at ideas and code, not at people
- Acknowledgement that maintainers and contributors volunteer their time

**We do not tolerate:**
- Personal attacks or insults
- Deliberately disruptive behaviour
- Spam or off-topic promotion

## Scope

This standard applies in all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community channels linked from this repository.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this standard, report it by opening a private GitHub issue or contacting the maintainers directly. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban accounts that repeatedly violate this standard.

---

*This project is built and maintained by people who care about good software and good communities. Be excellent to each other.*
